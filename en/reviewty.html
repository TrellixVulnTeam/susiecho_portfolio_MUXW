Overview

Brief

Reviewty is an E-commerce service specializing in cosmetics based on cosmetics information, user reviews, and community to improve the experience of buying and selling cosmetics in the Vietnamese market. It provides accurate cosmetic information to users who want to purchase cosmetics. And it serves as a new marketing tool for cosmetic sellers.

Task

I need to understand the problems of each stakeholder in Vietnam's rapidly growing cosmetic market and design services to solve them. The most significant goal of the solution is to help consumers find information about the products they want and improve their purchasing experience. Moreover, allowing sellers to promote their stores while providing information resources on this platform.

Problem

In Vietnam, the consumption of cosmetics is also growing along with the continuous increase in Vietnamese income. The cosmetics market is growing at an astonishing rate every year. However, consumers and sellers who are the key players in the E-commerce market have been having frictions of the following difficulties.

Consumer

  • Difficult to quickly reach the exact information of the desired product.

  • Same cosmetics but sold at different prices.

  • Too many fake cosmetics whose safety has not been confirmed.

Seller

  • Hard to inform my store to new consumers.

  • Difficult to improve the credibility of my store and cosmetics sold.

  • Sales and marketing channels are limited.

Goal

  • Provide a product that allows users to quickly reach the information they need when purchasing cosmetics.

  • Would make this product possible to be a must-check app before purchasing cosmetics.

  • Would create to be a forum to share cosmetic safety, ingredients, and correct usage.

  • Design so that both consumers and sellers can voluntarily and continuously exchange necessary information.

  • Provides continuous improvement by accumulating and analyzing users' usage behavior data.

Proposal

We aim to improve the cosmetics shopping experience of Vietnamese consumers and provide the Reviewty mobile app service that will offer a new marketing experience to sellers. The Reviewty app continuously improves and offers information and functions suitable for users by utilizing user logs and data accumulated as they use the service.

It will offer users a satisfying purchasing experience using reliable information and it will be a marketing tool that will become a new competitive edge to sellers. Moreover, It will help the Vietnamese cosmetics market to grow healthy by protecting its value to itself and the value of the sales store.

Empathise

Hypotheses

Due to I lived in Vietnam and have experienced difficulties in purchasing cosmetics, I was able to empathize with the problem directly. Regarding the selling side, by visiting the sales environment and observing the process before starting this project, acquired basic background knowledge and understood their difficulties what they have been going through.

Before conducting the research, To define the core of the problem and obtain undiscovered needs and insights, I assumed predictable users' frustrations and needs which occur when purchasing and selling in the Vietnamese cosmetic market.

Research

Approach

Closely observe the seller and retail stores' working process, to identify problems and needs. In this research, I was able to analyze the users' behaviors in general purchasing methods and live broadcast streaming purchasing methods and also was able to refer to VOC data.

We took a survey from 56 women in the 20s - 40s age group, who are in the main customer groups of the market, who have purchased products online within the past 3months.

A one-on-one interview was conducted with five people in each age group were selected from the same group as the target of the quantitative survey. In addition, we interviewed five retail stores over the phone or in person.

I researched user behaviors that have been researched along with the Vietnamese cosmetics market and competitors. For additional research needs, I referred to literature published by survey institutions and trade associations.

Data Modeling Resources

    Market

  • The size of the unofficial market sold through SNS channels such as Facebook and Instagram is estimated to be over 5 billion, and it grows every year rapidly.
  • Of the sample, 92% use Facebook, 18% use Zalo, and 12% use Instagram.
  • The Vietnamese cosmetics market has grown at an average annual growth rate of about 15% from 2013 to 2018.

    Consumer

  • Millennial women aged 23-39, who make up 68% of cosmetics buyers, spend the most on cosmetics.
  • The purchase rate through the smartphone app is the highest at 67%.
  • Consumers answered that it is difficult and takes a long time to find cosmetics and ingredients fit for them among numerous products.
  • Among the users who completed the purchase, 62% answered that they searched an average of 4 channels to obtain information about the desired product.

    Seller

  • The page that runs their account for using Facebook sales channel or live broadcasting streaming channel suddenly disappears. All interviewees answered that they had experienced.
  • The primary factor in choosing a marketing tool is price.
  • As a marketing tool, Facebook is essential and is done regularly. But after a certain period, the effect of new inflows decreases significantly.
  • Services such as brand websites are relatively expensive to create and have high technical barriers.

Here are some key insights through this process:

  • In the survey, 68% of Vietnamese women aged 18-38 said they felt a high sense of self-fulfillment and satisfaction through social media communication.

  • Cosmetic information translated through machine translation is not preferred because the expression is ambiguous and difficult to understand.

  • Cosmetic ads made as images are not even machine-translated, so consumers depend on influencers or sellers.

  • In the case of different prices of the same product, consumers answered that the brand credibility of the influencer or seller influences the purchase decision more than the price.

  • In sales through live broadcasting, consumers actively collect information through Q&A. However, this system makes the information volatilized, hard to search for specific information. Meanwhile sellers are getting tired from repeating answers.

As of 2018, according to ITU data, the penetration rate of desktops is 14.96%, and the rate of mobile phones is 147.2%, showing a big difference.

Therefore, it would be more efficient to develop the Reviewty as a mobile application first.

Classify & Develop

Ideas, expanded in the mind map process, are grouped into solvable issues. Convincingly define how these ideas will develop and how they will solve the problems.

How to get accurate information quickly?

  • Search engine optimization : Continue to optimize search engines such as detailed categories, part numbers and model names, automatic search word recommendations, similar information provision, search query storage, filters, and SEO management.

  • Image search : When takes a picture of the cosmetic label, offer the search result by reading a text.

  • User personalization: Compose the contents on some key pages by using personal data and logs.

  • Community a.k.a forum : Volatilized information in live broadcasting can be archived. And it will be helpful when users make their judgments by utilizing the collective intelligence accumulated through questions and answers.

  • Offer high-quality translated contents : It provides high-quality translation of information that helps consumers make purchase decisions, such as brand official information, ingredients, imaged advertisement information, etc, through the Reviewty translation service.

How to activate the community and review writing?

  • Compensation system : Offer expert badges for each level according to activities and evaluations. Rewards will give according to the amount of input information.

  • Review writing UX : Increase participation by providing various options for review writing methods such as satisfaction selection, Likert scale, and text. Induce high-quality reviews with differential rewards according to participation.

  • Initial community operation and management : By recruiting experts from the local makeup academy and the beauty community, we provide high-quality information and operate a 100% feedback guarantee system to accumulate quality and quality data.

  • Review credibility : A review monitoring system, self-authentication, and review policy. A blind system, exposed after completing the review inspection. Purchase authentication, personal emotional expression review and help evaluation, photo review, and black-list management.

How can we attract users?

  • Event·Coupon : Through participatory promotional events, we obtain the desired data. And offer reasonable compensation.

  • Level System : Offer interest by providing a level system like a game. Expand the reward compensation according to the level.

  • Community : In addition to official information, it provides information accumulated through collective intelligence formed through participation in Q&A and sharing.

  • Search query optimization and web services : Configure the web data so that when searching the web in Vietnamese, to reveal at the top of the Reviewty offered information. Additionally, build a website to find out about skin type.

  • Notification : Provides a notification function not only basic but also to possible to set keywords, to continuously induce reintroduction.

How can we powerfully communicate users' store or brand?

  • Store : Provide store lists in the integrated search, and display in order according to the activity of the store owner. On the store page, offer so sales, authenticate the genuine, and live broadcasting.

  • Level System : A level is given based on store activity. And design the difference in level. Exposure and frequency. Using tags to give different visual attention to the users.

  • Expose on arrival page after verifying genuine : After authenticated if the cosmetic is genuine, exposed a high-level store page on the arrival page by level.

  • Event·Coupon : Reviewty selected stores periodically according to ranking and level. And then held the event for users to increase the store reach and purchase rate.

How can we improve the credibility of a seller and the products they sell?

  • Store Review : Provides a store review function that writes after completing the purchase. Users can evaluate the store. Leverage user experience to increase trust.

  • Activity history and level : Expose the seller's community activities on the store page and provides expert-level tags. Users can check the professionalism and continuity of the store.

  • Genuine sales certification : By providing genuine sales authentication tags, ensure that consumers can purchase products with confidence. And raise the credibility of sellers and products sold.

Product Detail

01

How can it helps consumers quickly reach
the exact products and information they want?

It is not easy to find the cosmetic information that I am looking for

It provides an integrated search to reach the information users need. Such as high-level contextual searches such as categories and brands, highly autonomous keyword searches related to products such as product names and product codes. Moreover, detailed searches provide results that other users mention, such as reviews and feeds.

I don't know what to choose from the pouring cosmetics

Using accumulated data such as product search and category or brand viewing frequency, wish list, purchase data, age, and skin type. It recommends products with high purchase probability and interest to users on key screens.

I have to visit many sites to find enough information

Group and organize appropriately from essential information that users check to reach a purchase to additional information that will help them make a value judgment. It is designed to shorten the time to find information and recognize the product value for deciding to buy easier.

02

How can it give credibility to the product and selling price?

It's the same product, but different sellers have different prices. Moreover, I can't confirm whether they sell genuine products.

It improves the user's shopping experience by providing at-a-glance comparison and confirmation of the selling price, certificates the product authenticity, and shipping information of the selected product of all stores which sell.

I can't confirm whether the product I purchased is genuine or not.

After recognizing the QR of the authenticity sticker attached to the cosmetic, it is designed so that the authenticity can be checked by entering the authentication code. Through this, the anxiety of consumers can be relieved, and the seller can increase the credibility of the store.

Evaluation

Evaluation

We evaluated usability with the help of 'dbd lab', a UX usability evaluation company. 7 women aged 19-28 years and 4 women aged 29-38 years people evaluated for the selected topic. Although it received good evaluations such as simple, understandable, and usable without background knowledge in most of the items. However, there are some issues needed to be corrected and improved as follows.

Core Feedback

  • If the desired product is not registered, the function to report is required

  • Several Vietnamese labels on the product cause confusion

  • The high degree of autonomy in filter in the search makes low usability due to many options

Feedbacks that are found from usability tests and internal tests were added to the development requirements. I organized priorities based on the goals of the product we designed. And I scheduled them in weekly sprints to proceed with further development.
