Overview

Brief

Buskingplay is a matching platform service that matches performers and space owners. This service offers a safe place to perform to performers. And, it helps the space owners to utilize the space as marketing through performances.

This article is a study case of Buskingplay 2016 project. Please check the Buskingplay 2020-2021 project.

The Task

By operating the service as a Minimum Viable Product, users' needs and the size of the business have been continuously growing. Participate in product expansion by exploring the discovering needs of users and designing solutions to resolve users' frustrations. Then, design the defined items to the proper form, and then provide to apply smoothly them to app development by the developers.

Problem

Buskingplay was created to enhance the value of performance and busking culture by connecting performers and space owners. The business is growing but the product has the following problems.

  • The unsuitable platform for the user's usage behavior and the functions it provides

  • Low extension flexibility of existing development languages

  • The MVP level product that can not accommodate the expanded needs of users

  • The non-smooth user experience until reach the ultimate goal

  • Lack of Brand identity and aesthetics of the product

Goal

  • To discover new and expanded needs of users and present suitable solutions to them

  • To convey the ultimate concept of Buskingplay and expand the service

  • To secure development scalability by developing as a native app

  • To offer users with a highly usable, mobile-optimized product so that users can have a satisfying experience.

Proposal

Present the Buskingplay native app with improved functionality and usability rather than the MVP mobile web service. It can provide users with an improved busking matching experience and enables continuous and efficient development of the service that Buskingplay ultimately intends to offer.

Design Process

The design process is as follows. Even after completing the final test based on the planned development range, we had improved the product steadily to satisfy users by defining users' needs continuously, finding problems and improvements.

Empathise

Assumption

Before the research, established the needs of users discovered in the existing MVP service as the hypothesis as follows.

Research

The research approach is as follows.

1. User Research

Continuously conducted 1:1 interviews with general users and affiliate users of MVP products. Understand the problems that users want to solve, the inconveniences in the current situation, and their overall busking experience.

Invited users to observe how they use the product and whether users use it in an intended way or using differently. And found insights into where issues arise and where we can make improvements.

Visited the busking zone and observed the usage of the product. Identified inconveniences and necessities arising from outdoor use of the product, and interviewed and observed all stakeholders in the outside field continuously.

2. Service study

Analyzed existing products to solve the problem, and the focused investigation items are as follows.

Service goals

BM and results

UX·UI analysis

Operation issues

There are some key insights that we found as follow:

  • Due to the existing development method, there is a limit to the development and expansion of necessary functions.

  • There are users with different purposes for using the service.

  • Content published by the company cannot be exposed and accumulated in the product.

  • In the existing structure, there are restrictions on the expansion of new business models.

  • The usability is low due to the complicated UX·UI that is not clear.

  • Lack of brand identity leads to low aesthetics and reliability.

Define

Verified Assumption

User frustrations

  • Inconvenient to use the mobile web because location-based search or sharing is not available.

  • Hard to know the updated information such as the booking status and interest busking zone.

  • Complicated to complete my profile to a state where busking matching is possible.

User needs

  • Want a service that is easy to use from outside, regardless of location.

  • Want to get a notification of the updated status of the booking process and the busking zone they are interested in.

  • Want that using the service such as booking and payment settlement is easy and does not take much time.

Target

We redefine the target to make the service more sophisticated. Target A, which was not previously defined, was added.

Target A. General users who want various performances

  • People in their early 20s to late 30s who are highly interested in cultural performances

  • The relatively high proportion of women

  • Familiar with and interested in IT services

Target B. General users who want to perform more

  • Music and non-music performers in their late 10s to mid-40s

  • There is no significant difference in the male to female ratio

  • Not familiar on and low interest in IT services

Platform

Based on the research, we discussed what is the most suitable development environment to solve problems and what language and platform to build on. Determined iOS and Android native apps to be the most appropriate for continuous business expansion and continuous implementation of functions to meet user needs. It will be a suitable platform to improve the quality of service experience and secure development scalability.

Scope

Buskingplay service consists of the following three products.

  • Buskingplay is used by general users and performers

  • The zone admin website used by spatial affiliates

  • The integrated admin website to operate both services

We decided to develop Buskingplay first that most efficiently reveals the direction the service seeks and can most effectively solve the problems experienced by many users.

Although I participated in the admin site development and the admin site for affiliate users, this study case doesn't cover it.

Verified Problem

We verified the hypothesis through the research process, identified the user's frustrations and needs, and defined the problems to be solved.

✅  Services provided as the MVP-level product

  • Need to grow into a product that can meet user needs over the minimum viable product

  • Need to develop additional functions that can reveal the direction the service is pursuing

✅  Development language with low extensibility flexibility

  • Secure development manpower by using a relatively common development language

  • Gain flexibility to expand the service development

✅  Usability not carefully considered

  • Need to upgrade the user experience of main processes such as membership registration, information input, and booking

  • Remove user confusion by providing notifications in the right place and times, and improve to clear UX writing

✅  Lack of Brand identity and aesthetics

  • Improving brand identity to increase service credibility and awareness

  • Utilize brand colors and consistent design to enhance product integrity and aesthetics

Ideate

Ideate

Classify & Develop

Classified the meaningful ideas discovered in the above process by topic and advanced to apply to development.

Service as a Minimum Viable Product   →   Expand functions of the product

  • Busking list search, advanced filter, and category classification

  • Enhance exposure and searching of the matched performance schedule, and exposure of Buskingplay provision content

  • Offers things to see and play with functions such as live broadcasting, in-app payment, and tips

  • Watch and download performance videos uploaded by users

  • Chat function for performers and spatial affiliate users

Lack of usability   →   Native app implementation and mobile optimization UX·UI

  • Optimize information that exposes a list of available performances space and performance schedules around users by utilizing the location function of the mobile device

  • Support social login, improvement of the membership registration process for performance player members

  • Improved booking process and added update notification function for progress after booking

Lack of visual aesthetics   →   Utilize and improvement brand identity

  • Improvement of existing brand identity

  • Brand identity and consistent product design

02

Hard to find the busking information,
How can get the information quickly and accurately?

Busking schedule at a glance

General users can easily monitor and search for new performance information. Player users can check and promote their busking schedule through this.

Busking performance information and benefits check in advance

Offer the function to find at once that the scatter busking information on the artist's SNS or had to be met by chance on the road. In addition, possible to check in advance the benefits you get from Busking Zone.

03

I don't like complicated things,
Can book and manage performances simply and easily?

Available booking busking zones list

The existing service provided a list in alphabetical order, develop to offer a list of available booking busking zones to help reach users' goal faster. In addition, provides regional and brand tabs reflected users' search topics in the previous product.

Choose a time with no degrees of freedom

To reduce users' confusion and to simplify schedule management of spatial affiliate users, remove the autonomy of selectable performance times and set the minimum performance time. In addition, designed to check available booking schedules at a glance.
