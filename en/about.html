Hi there, I'm Susie.

Hello, this is a product designer, Susie Cho. First of all, thank you for visiting my space.

I have experienced in product design in Korea from 2015 to 2017 and Vietnam from 2017 to 2020. After coming to Korea, I planned the gap year at the beginning of the 6th year of this field. Then, I am currently spending the last quarter of the gap year. Now, I am standing at the start line with full energy.

I like the process of listening to and empathizing with the problem, finding the answer to the problem. Moreover, I'm fond of creating results by thinking about the 'why' and 'how'. And I love how the multiple people involved in this process work together towards the best outcome in an efficient way.

I like to think of myself as a full-stack product designer who can collaborate and lead from the research and design to the evaluation phase of a project and make it seamless for developers to develop.
